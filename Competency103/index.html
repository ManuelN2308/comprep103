Top 5 Favorite Video Games

Final Fantasy 7

Final Fantasy 7 follows the story of mercenary Cloud Strife, who is hired by the eco-terrorist group AVALANCHE—led by Barret Wallace—to help fight the mega-corporation Shinra Electric Power Company, who attempts to drain the planet's lifeblood as an energy source to further their profits.

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim is an action role-playing game, playable from either a first or third-person perspective. The player may freely roam over the land of Skyrim which is an open world environment consisting of wilderness expanses, dungeons, cities, towns, fortresses, and villages.

Fallout: New Vegas

Fallout: New Vegas takes place in the year 2281. Players take control of a character known as the Courier and travel the vast wasteland of the Mojave.

Final Fantasy 8

Final Fantasy VIII consists of three main modes of play: the world map, the field map, and the battle screen. The world map is a 3D display in which the player may navigate freely across a small-scale rendering of the game world.

Ark: Survival Evolved

Ark: Survival Evolved is an action-adventure survival video game. The game can be played in both single-player and multiplayer game modes. Multiplayer allows the option to form tribes of members in a server.
